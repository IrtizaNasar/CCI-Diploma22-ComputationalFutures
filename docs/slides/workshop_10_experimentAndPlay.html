Hi!
Welcome to our tenth experiment/play session!
By the end of this workshop, you'll have done the following things:
  • Experimented with the openFrameworks forum
  • Played with the openFrameworks website learning section
  • Experimented with openFrameworks book
  • Played with the ofxAddons site
  • Experimented with other openFrameworks resources for learning
  • Downloaded Apple xCode or Microsoft Visual Studio (depending if you are on OS X or Windows) then tried downloading openFrameworks and compiling some of the supplied examples
Don't worry about understanding all the code you'll see today.
Experiment with the oF forum for 5 minutes in groups. What can you find? Try searching for things you are interested in.
Play with the oF website learning section for 5 minutes - I always have this open when I'm writing code.
Experiment with the oF Book for 10 minutes - which chapters seem interesting? Why?
Play with the ofxAddons site for 5 minutes. Which addons might be useful for your practice?
Experiment with the Resources for Learning section of the openFrameworks wiki for 5 minutes. I'd particuarly recommend some of the video tutorials further down the list.
I'd like you take 30 minutes to follow the four parts of the "Getting Started!" section of the learning section of the oF website. Shout me on the CCI Slack if you are stuck.
Thanks!
